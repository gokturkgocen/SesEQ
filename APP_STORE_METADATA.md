# Eqlume App Store Metadata

## English

**Name:** Eqlume

**Subtitle:** EQ that follows your music

**Promotional text:** System-wide sound shaping with automatic, genre-aware profiles — built natively for Apple silicon Macs.

**Description:**

Eqlume is a lightweight system-wide equalizer that lives in your Mac's menu bar. It processes audio in real time and can automatically choose a sound profile that fits what you are listening to.

OPEN IT ONCE. LET IT FOLLOW THE MUSIC.

Automatic mode reads now-playing details from supported music apps and selects a fitting profile for rock, electronic, hip-hop, jazz, classical, pop, ambient, and more.

BUILT FOR THE MAC AUDIO PATH

Eqlume uses native Core Audio processing with no kernel extension or virtual audio driver. A live response graph and spectrum view show what is happening at a glance.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Audio is processed in memory on your Mac. It is never recorded, uploaded, or sent to the developer. Online catalog lookups use track and artist text only. Spotify integration is optional.

FEATURES

• System-wide real-time equalization
• Automatic genre-aware sound profiles
• Live spectrum and EQ response visualization
• Spotify, Apple Music, and supported browser integrations
• Previous, play/pause, and next controls
• Automatic bypass for unsupported outputs
• English and Turkish interface
• No ads or behavioral tracking

Eqlume currently applies its tuned correction when using the Mac's built-in 3.5 mm headphone output. Other outputs are bypassed safely.
